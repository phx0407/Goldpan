How It Works

Simple Process.
No Work on Your End.

01
We Score Your Dishes
We map every dish ingredient by ingredient and score each one on a 100-point transparency scale across four dimensions. No guessing. No inference.
02
You See Your Results
Before anything goes live, you see exactly how your dishes scored and what the data shows. Your score is a starting point, not a verdict.
03
You Make Your Claims
Tell us what your restaurant stands for. The qualities you are proud of, the commitments you make to your customers, the things you want diners to know before they walk in.
04
You Confirm Your Ingredients
A simple confirmation process locks in your data and raises your score. When you confirm, your Goldpan Verified badge goes on and your dishes move up in results.
05
The Right Diners Find You
Customers filtering by allergen, dietary need, or restaurant claim see your dishes first. Your restaurant becomes the answer to the question they are already asking.
