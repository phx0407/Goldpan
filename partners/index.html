How It Works

Five Steps.
No Work on Your End.

01
Say Yes
You confirm your founding partner status. That is all we need to get started. No paperwork, no commitment beyond the conversation.
02
We Map Your Menu
We break down your dishes ingredient by ingredient. Protein, base, sauces, seasonings. Exactly as you describe them. No inference. No guessing.
03
We Score Your Dishes
Every dish gets a Transparency Score from 0 to 100 across four dimensions. We show you the results before anything goes live.
04
You Confirm Your Ingredients
A simple confirmation process. Fast and easy for your team. When you confirm, your score goes up and your Goldpan Verified badge goes on.
05
The Right Diners Find You
Customers filtering by allergen or dietary need see your dishes first. Your restaurant becomes the answer to the question they are already asking.
