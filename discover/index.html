Data Accuracy. Dish and ingredient information reflects what was publicly disclosed at the time of canvassing. Goldpan does not guarantee that information is current or complete.

Transparency Levels. Goldpan's transparency levels (Building, Moderate, High) measure how clearly a restaurant discloses what is in its food — not food quality, safety, or healthfulness. A higher level means more disclosure, not a health endorsement.
