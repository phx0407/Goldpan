// The Problem

Menus Hide More Than They Show

You have a food allergy, a dietary preference, or you just want to know what you're putting in your body. But restaurant menus are built for selling — not for telling.

Sauces without ingredients. "Seasonal vegetables" with no specifics. Allergen information buried or missing entirely. Every meal is a gamble.

⚠️
Hidden Allergens
Common allergens buried inside compound sauces, dressings, and coatings with no disclosure.
🌫️
Vague Dietary Claims
"Gluten-free" and "vegan" labels with no verification or ingredient-level evidence behind them.
🔍
No Accountability Layer
Diners have no way to know how confident they should be in any given menu claim.
