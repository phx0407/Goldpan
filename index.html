The Opportunity

Some Restaurants Tell You Everything. We Help You Find Them.

There are restaurants that are transparent. Ones that tell you exactly what is in your food, how it is prepared, and what allergens to watch for. They deserve to be found.

Goldpan scores every dish on ingredient transparency so diners with dietary needs, allergies, or simply a desire to know what they are eating can find the restaurants that care.

Transparent Restaurants Win
Restaurants that disclose their ingredients get recognized, discovered, and trusted by the diners who care most.
Diners Get Confidence
Filter by allergen or dietary need and instantly see which dishes are clear and how confident you can be in that answer.
Trust Goes Both Ways
A Goldpan score is not a grade. It is a signal. Restaurants that earn it build stronger relationships with their most loyal customers.
